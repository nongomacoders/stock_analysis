1.    INTRODUCTION




1.1         Shareholders of Ethos Capital ("Ethos Capital Shareholders") are referred to the


            declaration announcement released by the Company on the Stock Exchange News


            Service operated by JSE Limited on Tuesday, 18 November 2025 (the "Declaration


            Announcement"), wherein Ethos Capital Shareholders were advised that the board of


            directors of Ethos Capital (the "Board") had decided (based on the recommendation of


            the Investment Advisor) to unbundle the remaining portion of Ethos Capital's


            investment in Brait plc ("Brait"), comprising of the senior unsecured exchangeable


            registered bonds, held indirectly by Ethos Capital through the Ethos Direct Investments


            Fund and Ethos Fund VII, in Brait Investment Holdings Limited, a wholly owned


            subsidiary of Brait, on behalf of Ethos Capital as a limited partner (the "Brait Bonds") to


            Ethos Capital Shareholders (the "2025 Brait Unbundling").




1.2         The purpose of this announcement is to provide Ethos Capital Shareholders with the


            finalisation information in respect of the 2025 Brait Unbundling in accordance with the


            JSE Listings Requirements, now that the conditions to the 2025 Brait Unbundling have


            been fulfilled.




1.3         All capitalised terms used but not defined in this announcement shall bear the meanings


            ascribed to them in the Declaration Announcement.




2.    THE 2025 BRAIT UNBUNDLING




      Ethos Capital Shareholders are reminded that, as set out in the Declaration Announcement:




2.1         the 2025 Brait Unbundling will be implemented as a capital distribution in specie to


            Ethos Capital Shareholders of, in aggregate, 222 204 Brait Bonds (valued at R175.5


            million as at 30 June 2025), each with a nominal value of R750 (R250 per bond capital


            repayment made in August 2024) and maturing on 3 December 2027, and will result in


            Ethos Capital distributing 0.00086 Brait Bonds (under JSE alpha code BIHLEB and ISIN


            MU0707E00002), for every 1 Ethos Capital A ordinary share ("Ethos Capital Share")


            held by Ethos Capital Shareholders recorded in the register on the record date, being


            Friday, 5 December 2025;




2.2         Ethos Capital Shareholders who transfer or dispose of their Ethos Capital Shares prior


            to the last day to trade in Ethos Capital Shares in order to be eligible to participate in


            the 2025 Brait Unbundling, being Tuesday, 2 December 2025, will not participate in the


            2025 Brait Unbundling and will not receive the Brait Bonds; and




2.3         the 2025 Brait Unbundling will not involve an election on the part of Ethos Capital


            Shareholders and, accordingly, no Ethos Capital Shareholder shall be entitled to elect


            to receive cash in lieu of their entitlement to receive the Brait Bonds in terms of the 2025


            Brait Unbundling.




3.    FULFILMENT OF CONDITIONS TO THE 2025 BRAIT UNBUNDLING




      The Company is pleased to announce that as of today, 25 November 2025, the 2025 Brait


      Unbundling has become unconditional as the Company has received the requisite consent


      from FirstRand Bank Limited (acting through its Rand Merchant Bank division) ("RMB") to


      proceed with the implementation of the 2025 Brait Unbundling and RMB has accordingly


      released the Brait Bonds from the ambit of the security previously attaching thereto.

5.    FRACTIONAL ENTITLEMENTS




      Ethos Capital Shareholders are reminded that, as set out in the Declaration Announcement:




5.1         the distribution of the Brait Bonds to Ethos Capital Shareholders in accordance with the


            distribution entitlement ratio set out above, could result in fractional entitlements for


            such Ethos Capital Shareholders, which fractional entitlements will be rounded down to


            the nearest whole number and aggregated;




5.2         the aggregated excess fractional entitlements to the Brait Bonds to which Ethos Capital


            Shareholders would otherwise be entitled will not be transferred to Ethos Capital


            Shareholders, but will instead be sold on behalf of and at the risk of the relevant Ethos


            Capital Shareholders in the market as soon as practicable following the implementation


            of the 2025 Brait Unbundling. Ethos Capital Shareholders will accordingly be paid the


            cash in respect of the fractional entitlements to the unbundled Brait Bonds, on the basis


            outlined in the Declaration Announcement; and




5.3         the cash value to be used to calculate the Cash Proceeds in respect of the fractional


            entitlements will be announced before 11h00 (SAST) on Thursday, 4 December 2025,


            in accordance with the JSE Listings Requirements.
