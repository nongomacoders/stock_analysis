SECOND QUARTER OPERATIONAL UPDATE ? FY2026




Cashbuild herewith provides its voluntary quarterly operational update for the second quarter of the 2026


financial year ("second quarter"). The update includes the revenue results of Allbuildco Holdings (Pty) Ltd


(trading as Amper Alles), a subsidiary acquired with a 60% controlling interest, with effect from 1 December


2025.




Revenue for the second quarter for the Group increased by 1% when compared to the second quarter of the


prior financial year ("the comparative period"). For the 309 existing stores (in existence prior to July 2024),


revenue decreased by 2% and the 15 new stores, contributed 3% growth. This, combined with the results


reported in the first quarter of the 2026 financial year, equates to an increase in revenue for the half year ended


28 December 2025 ("half year" or "HY2026") of 3% when compared to the prior half year ended 29


December 2024 ("HY2025").




Transactions through the tills during the second quarter for the Group increased by 2% (half year: 4%) when


compared to the comparative period, with existing stores decreasing by 1% (half year: increasing by 2%) and


new stores contributing 3% (half year: 2%).




Selling inflation was 0.8% at the end of December 2025 compared to HY2025.

#P&L Hardware and Amper Alles




During the second quarter, the Group opened 1 new store (half year: 4), refurbished 7 stores (half year: 8),


relocated 1 store (half year: 1) and closed 1 under-performing store (half year: 1). The Group further acquired


3 Amper Alles stores, and disposed of its interest in the Malawi operations (2 stores) and bringing the total


number of stores trading at half year end to 322.




The information contained in this operational update has not been reviewed or reported on by Cashbuild's


auditors.
