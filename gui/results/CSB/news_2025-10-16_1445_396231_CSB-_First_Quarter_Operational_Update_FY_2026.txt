FIRST QUARTER OPERATIONAL UPDATE ? FY 2026




Cashbuild herewith provides its voluntary quarterly operational update for the first quarter of the 2026


financial year ("first quarter").




Revenue for the first quarter for the Group increased by 6% compared to the first quarter of the prior financial


year ("the comparative period"). For the 310 existing stores (in existence prior to July 2024), revenue


increased by 4% and the 11 new stores, contributed 2% growth. Transactions through the tills increased by 6%


to that of the comparative period, with existing stores increasing by 4% and new stores increasing by 2%.




Selling inflation was 1.4% at the end of September 2025 when compared to September 2024.
