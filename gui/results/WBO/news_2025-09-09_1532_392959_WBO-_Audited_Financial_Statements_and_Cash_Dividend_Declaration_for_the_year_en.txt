AUDITED FINANCIAL STATEMENTS AND CASH DIVIDEND DECLARATION FOR THE YEAR ENDED 30 JUNE 2025


FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE:


The Group performed well for the financial year ended 30 June 2025 demonstrating improved margins from


most business units.


Key highlights:


    - Revenue from continuing operations increased by 4% to R28.5 billion


      (2024: R27.5 billion)


    - Operating profit increased by 13% to R1.4 billion (2024: R1.2 billion)


    - Earnings per share from continuing operations improved to 2 299 cents (2024: 1 853 cents)


    - Headline earnings from continuing operations per share increased to 2 278 cents (2024: 2 021 cents)


    - Earnings per share from total operations improved to 2 357 cents (2024: 1 844 cents)


    - Headline earnings from total operations per share increased to 2 337 cents (2024: 2 012 cents)


    - The net asset value of the Group amounts to R5.6 billion (2024: R4.7 billion)


    - Order book of R37.6 billion at June 2025 (2024: R30.6 billion)


    - Final cash dividend of 320 cents per share declared for the year ended 30 June 2025


      (2024: 230 cents per share)
