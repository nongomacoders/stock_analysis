Notice of an acquisition of beneficial interest in WBHO securities by Foord Balanced Fund


In accordance with section 122 of the Companies Act, 71 of 2008 ("the Act"), and section 3.83(b) of


the JSE Listings Requirements, shareholders are advised that WBHO has received formal notification,


in terms of section 122(1)(a) of the Act that Foord Balanced Fund has acquired a beneficial interest in


the Company, such that the total interest in the ordinary shares of the Company held by Foord


Balanced Fund now amounts to 5.01% of the total issued ordinary shares of the Company.


As required in terms of section 122(3) (a) of the Act, WBHO has filed the required notice with the


Takeover Regulation Panel.


The board of directors accepts responsibility for the information contained in this announcement, and


to the best of their knowledge and belief, that information is true, and this announcement does not


omit anything likely to affect the importance of the information included.
