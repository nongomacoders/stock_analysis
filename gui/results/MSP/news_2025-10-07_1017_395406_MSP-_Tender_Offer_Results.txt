On 29 September 2025, stakeholders were advised that MAS Securities BV (the "Issuer"), a wholly owned subsidiary of


the Company, announced its invitation to holders of the EUR300,000,000 4.25 per cent. Guaranteed Notes due 2026 (ISIN:


XS2339025277) (the "Notes") to tender such Notes for purchase by the Issuer for cash (the "Offer") in an aggregate


principal amount up to the Maximum Acceptance Amount, on the terms and subject to the conditions set out in the tender


offer memorandum dated 29 September 2025 (the "Tender Offer Memorandum") prepared by the Issuer. The Offer


remains subject to the offer and distribution restrictions set out below and as more fully described in the Tender Offer


Memorandum. Capitalised terms used in this announcement but not defined have the meanings given to them in the Tender


Offer Memorandum.


The Expiration Deadline for the Offer was 4.00 p.m. (London time) on 6 October 2025. The Company today announces that


the Issuer will accept for purchase EUR 119,998,000 in aggregate principal amount of the Notes validly tendered pursuant


to the Offer. The total purchase consideration (including Accrued Interest Payments) for Notes validly tendered and


accepted for purchase pursuant to the Offer will be EUR 121,982,076.41.
