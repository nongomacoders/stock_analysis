SHORT FORM ANNOUNCEMENT: AUDITED FINANCIAL RESULTS FOR THE YEAR ENDED 30


JUNE 2025


1.   SALIENT FEATURES


GPI reported a headline profit of R65.9 million for the financial year ended 30 June 2025, a decrease


of R16.5 million (20%) from earnings of R82.4 million for the prior corresponding period.


Headline earnings per share decreased by 20% to 15.42 cents per share from earnings of 19.20 cents


per share in the prior corresponding period.


Basic earnings per share decreased by 16% to 14.32 cents per share, from 17.14 cents per share in


the prior corresponding period.


2.   DIVIDEND


The board of directors of the Company ("Board") has taken a decision not to declare a final dividend


in respect of the year ended 30 June 2025 (30 June 2024: 11.5 cents).
