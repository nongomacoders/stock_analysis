NOTIFICATION IN TERMS OF SECTION 122(3) OF THE COMPANIES ACT AND PARAGRAPH


3.83(B) OF THE JSE LISTINGS REQUIREMENTS


In accordance with section 122(3)(b) of the Companies Act, No. 71 of 2008, and section 3.83(b) of


the JSE Listings Requirements, shareholders are hereby advised that Cell C has received formal


notification that clients of Allan Gray Proprietary Limited ("Allan Gray") have, in aggregate, acquired


an interest in the ordinary shares of the Company, such that the total interest in the ordinary shares


of the Company held by Allan Gray's clients now amounts to 15.5383% of the total issued ordinary


shares of the Company.


As required in terms of section 122(3)(a) of the Act, the Company has filed the required notice with


the Takeover Regulation Panel.


The board of directors of the Company accepts responsibility for the information contained in this


announcement as it relates to the Company and confirms that, to the best of its knowledge and


belief, such information relating to the Company is true and correct, based on the accuracy of the


information received from Allan Gray Proprietary Limited and that this announcement does not omit


anything likely to affect the importance of such information.
