VOLUNTARY ANNOUNCEMENT: THE TENDER OFFER FOR THE 2026 SENIOR NOTES ISSUED BY THE


BIDVEST GROUP (UK) Plc


Bidvest, through its subsidiary The Bidvest Group (UK) Plc (the "Issuer"), has today released the tender


results in respect of the cash tender offer of the Issuer's 3.625% senior notes due 2026 (the "Notes") (the


"Tender Offer") launched on 8 September 2025. The Notes are guaranteed by Bidvest.


Further information has been made available to holders of the Notes.
