AUDITED ANNUAL FINANCIAL STATEMENTS FOR THE YEAR ENDED 30 JUNE 2025


1.   SALIENT FEATURES


     Revenue increased by 2% to R29 615 million, from R29 062 million for the year ended 30 June


     2024 ('prior year').


     Operating profit before capital items decreased by 14% to R1 937 million, from R2 250 million in


     the prior year.


     Headline earnings per share decreased by 47% to 24.1 cents, from 45.3 cents in the prior year.


     Earnings per share decreased by 99% to 0.4 cents, from 43.8 cents in the prior year.


     Net asset value per share decreased by 0.4% to 498 cents, from 500 cents in the prior year.


     The board of directors has not declared a dividend for the year ended 30 June 2025


     (30 June 2024: Nil).
