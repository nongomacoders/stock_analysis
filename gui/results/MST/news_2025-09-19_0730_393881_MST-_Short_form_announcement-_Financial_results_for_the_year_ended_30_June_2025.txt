SHORT FORM ANNOUNCEMENT: FINANCIAL RESULTS FOR THE YEAR ENDED 30 JUNE 2025


AND CASH DIVIDEND DECLARATION


SALIENT FEATURES:


- Revenue R7,2 billion, down 14.9% (2024 (restated): R8,4 billion)


- Gross profit % increased to 13,3% (2024 (restated): 12,2%)


- Headline earnings per share 72,73 cents, up 8,3% (2024: 67,13 cents)


- Basic earnings per share 71,71 cents, up 92,2% (2024: 37,31 cents)


- Dividend per share 13,75 cents, up 83,3% (2024: 7.5 cents)


- Net asset value per share 2 869,71 cents, up 2,4% (2024: 2 801,15 cents)


The prior year restatement is related to agency revenue that was


incorrectly classified as cost of sales as well as agency revenue related


costs classified as part of operating expenses. This error had no impact


on prior year profit. Refer to note 38 of the Annual Financial Statements.


The information has been extracted from the audited results for the year


ended 30 June 2025. This announcement itself has not been audited or


reviewed.

DIVIDEND DECLARATION


Notice is hereby given that a final cash dividend of 13.75 cents per


ordinary share for the year ended 30 June 2025 is declared, payable to


shareholders recorded in the books of the company at the close of business


on the record date appearing below. This dividend is declared out of


income reserves. The company's income tax reference number is 9550081716


and the company has 57 540 000 ordinary shares in issue and ranking for


dividend at the date of this declaration. The South African dividend tax


rate is 20% resulting in a net dividend of 11.0 cents per share to


shareholders who are not tax exempt.
