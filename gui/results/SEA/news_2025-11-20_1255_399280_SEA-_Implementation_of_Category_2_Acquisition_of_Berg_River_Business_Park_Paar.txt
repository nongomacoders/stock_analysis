IMPLEMENTATION OF CATEGORY 2 ACQUISITION OF BERG RIVER BUSINESS PARK, PAARL


Shareholders are referred to the announcements published on SENS on 21 May 2025 and 6 August 2025,


regarding the acquisition by Spear of the properties known as the Remainder of Erf 5400, the Remainder of


Erf 5401, the Remainder of Erf 5428, Erf 11204, Erf 11205 and Erf 13058, Paarl, in the Drakenstein


Municipality, with all improvements thereon ("Property"), and the rental enterprise known as the "Berg River


Business Park" conducted on the Property, as a going concern ("Acquisition"), for a purchase consideration


of R182 150 000.


Spear is pleased to advise that the implementation of the Acquisition was finalised on 20 November 2025


("Implementation Date"), on which date the transfer of ownership of the Property was registered into Spear's


name. On the Implementation Date, Spear's gross portfolio asset value increased to R6.39 billion, and Spear


had a market capitalisation of R4.87 billion. The Gross Lettable Area (GLA) of Spear's portfolio has now


increased to 542 662 m2 of high-quality Western Cape only assets. On the Implementation Date, Spear's Loan


to Value Ratio (LTV) is between 21% and 22%.
