IMPLEMENTATION OF CATEGORY 2 ACQUISITION OF MAYNARD MALL, WYNBERG


Shareholders are referred to the announcements published on SENS on 28 July 2025 and 3 September 2025,


regarding the acquisition of the properties known as the Remainder Erf 67925, Remainder Erf 67947,


Erf 67948, Remainder Erf 67949, Remainder Erf 67957, Remainder Erf 67959, Remainder Erf 67960,


Remainder Erf 67958, Erf 92087 and Remainder Erf 92089, in the City of Cape Town, with all improvements


thereon ("Property"), and the rental enterprise known as the "Maynard Mall" conducted by the seller on the


Property, as a going concern ("Acquisition"), for a purchase consideration of R455 000 000.


Spear is pleased to advise that the implementation of the Acquisition was finalised on 31 October 2025


("Implementation Date"), on which date the transfer of ownership of the Property was registered into Spear's


name.


On the Implementation Date, Spear's gross portfolio asset value increased to R6.2 billion, and Spear had a


market capitalisation of R4.6 billion. Spear's portfolio Gross Lettable Area (GLA) has now increased to


513 286 m² of high-quality Western Cape only assets. On the Implementation Date, Spear's Loan to Value


Ratio (LTV) is between 18% and 19%.
